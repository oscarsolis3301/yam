 Changes Scheduled for August 2025  
    
  
 
 
   
     
   
   
  
   
 
AHCCCS is committed to improving service to providers by keeping you informed of upcoming changes. Changes are being made to the AHCCCS Provider Enrollment Portal (APEP) effective 08/31/2025.
 

Who Is Impacted? 

All providers 
 

What Is Changing? 

Effective 08/31/2025 the following changes will be made in APEP and affect all providers:


When a provider is terminated, any pending modification requests will be returned to the provider’s queue. If this occurs, the provider may submit a reactivation modification request. ​

The billing association screen will be enhanced to include the legal entity AND Doing Business As name for the billing entity. 

Effective 08/31/2025, the following change will be made in APEP and affects provider types B8 Behavioral Health Residential Facility, CF Counseling Only Facility, IC Integrated Clinics, and 77 Behavioral Health Outpatient Clinic: 

New mandatory questions have been added to the application.
An electronic signature is required before the application can be submitted.

 

Additional Information 

Additional information will be provided closer to the actual effective date of the change.
