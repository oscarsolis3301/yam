Dear Dr. Wang, 


 


Delta Dental of Washington (DDWA) is excited to welcome you to the network. With almost 4 million members receiving dental coverage from us across Washington state and beyond, we look forward to partnering with you to fulfill
 our shared vision of helping all people enjoy good oral and overall health, with no one left behind.


 


We are proud to welcome you as a Member Dentist in our
Premier
 and PPO. Network, effective 
6/28/2025. Your membership includes the benefit of participating with 
Delta Dental Plans Association (DDPA). more than 80 million Americans through 39 independent companies, DDPA protects more smiles than any other dental benefits company, with coverage in all 50 states and other U.S. territories.


 


To make the most of your DDWA and DDPA membership:   



Create An Account on Our Provider Portal
(DeltaDentalWA.com/Provider) 
The DDWA Provider Portal is our primary information and self-service resource for network dentists and contains everything you and your practice needs to work with DDWA. From the Provider Portal you can: 



o   
Find forms and manuals 


o   
View current fees and claims 


o   
Review Member Dentist Rules and Regulations 


o   
Access real-time support with Online Chat 


o   
Explore our library of tools, blogs, recent communications, and clinical information.  



Explore Your Welcome Packet  
Download the attached Welcome Packet for practical resources and helpful tips on how to get the most out of the Provider Portal and your DDWA experience.   


Meet your Member Advisory Panel (MAP) 



The Member Advisory Panel are a group of Member Dentists who are elected to serve as advisory body to DDWA leadership to represent and help advocate for the Member Dentist experience. You can

learn more about the MAP on our Provider Portal and send your questions and feedback to
AskTheMAP@DeltaDentalWA.com.  



Just for Dentists: Text DENTIST to 56675 to get key updates with DenTEXT
 
DenTEXT is our dentist-only text channel for Member Dentists to get only the DDWA news and updates that matter most to dentists delivered right to your cell phone. Once you’ve joined, you can also text FEEDBACK anytime you want to give DDWA or the MAP fast
 feedback about your Member Dentist experience.  



We’re committed to providing personalized support and extraordinary service. Here’s how to quickly and easily contact our offices:  



Online Chat 



Get quick answers to your questions without having to pick up the phone. Chat with a service representative to get instant information regarding claim status, patient benefits, payment vouchers, and much more by logging into the
 Provider Portal and clicking the green chat icon in the bottom right corner.  


 



Provider Ambassadors 



Our Provider Ambassadors are here to support your best Member Dentist experience – and will be contacting you soon to answer any questions on your membership or doing business with DDWA. You can also contact our Provider Ambassador
 team any time you need personalized practice support by emailing 
Provider@DeltaDentalWA.com, along with adding this email to your contact list to ensure you receive important email announcements and updates.
